Budgeting 101: How Much House Can You Afford

A few notes before you get started

We have designed a budgeting tool that will help with estimating a monthly mortgage expense. Our hope is that you can use this information to help start your home buying experience. After you enter your budgeting information, you will be shown a remaining amount that can be applied as a monthly mortgage payment. Use the dropdowns on the mortgage calculator to change loan terms and you will be given potential homes that fall within your range of affordability. It should be noted that this site is used as a BASIC GUIDE and in no way should be used other than a starting estimation of what you can afford. Please consult with a licensed financial advisor, bank representative, or real estate agent for a true analysis of your financial situation.
