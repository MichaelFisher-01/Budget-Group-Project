Budgeting 101: How Much House Can You Afford

A few notes before you get started

We have designed a budgeting tool that will help with estimating various household costs. Our hope is that you can use this information to help start your home buying experience. After you enter your budgeting information, you will be shown an estimate of a total loan amount, interest rates in the current market, and various homes for sale that fall within that range. It should be noted that this site is used as a BASIC GUIDE and in no way should be used other than a basic starting estimation of what you can afford. Please consult with a licensed financial advisor, bank representative, or real estate agent for a true analysis of your financial situation.
